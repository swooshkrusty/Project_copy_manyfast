    Scan & Tag

    The MFS DATAENGINE searches millions of records for product information including:

               Size Variation
               Dimensions
               Product Specs
               Descriptions 
                   Product Names
               Retail Pricing
               Brand
               Mode

    Label products for auction planning (with lot/section) or for standard inventory. Each tag is unique to the product, so theres no risk of duplicate files or mishaps.

    Manage Catalog

    The MFS DATAENGINE will search for retail-ready product data and stock images, creating a 5-star photo gallery. After a UPC is scanned, just snap a photo from a webcam or mobile device and upload any additional information customers may need, such condition and completeness.

    Skip manual data entry and manage products quickly. The robust collection of data on each product and sale brings transparency to product, employee, and business performance. Improve process, performance, and SEO with data from the MFS DATAENGINE.
